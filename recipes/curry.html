Sweet and Spicy Red Lentil Curry

Description

When I was a young boi my mom always made this dish for me. It uses pantry ingredients, has a sweet tangy flavor, and is easy to store.

Anyone can make this recipe, and it's totally vegan. In fact, it's the dish I show people to convince them that a vegan lifestyle isn't as hard as they think. Try it!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried red lentils
  • 1 cup white rice
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • Spice Blend
  • 1 cup rice
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 tbsp ginger
  • 1/2 tbsp fresh turmeric
  • 1 thai green chili
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup fresh cilantro
  • 1 whole lime, quartered
  • 2 Cups Vegetable Broth

Steps

  1. In medium saucepan, add oil, spice blend, garlic, ginger, and diced green chili. Remove seeds if you're a bitch.
  2. Allow to cook on low to medium heat until fragrent (2-3 minutes tops - do not allow to burn)
  3. Add vegetable broth, bring to a boil on medium-high heat.
  4. Add lentils and reduce heat to simmer after 5 minutes
  5. Add tomato sauce, stir gently for 10 min. Add salt to taste.
  6. Add coconut milk
  7. Add diced cilantry and juice from lime. Server over white rice, or with Naan.
