Spicy Curry Make Me Purry

Description

This is a decent curry blend, which you can modify at your discretion. I use normal pantry spices for this, mixed with fresh aromatics.

You can use your own whole spices for this, or you can sub out many of these ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 big tablespoons Garam Massala
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon coriander
  • 1 tablespoon cumin powder
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 indian curry (madrassa is fine, can also just use extra garam masalla)
